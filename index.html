Fasting Tips

Before Fasting

• Eat healthy foods

• Drink plenty of water

• Do not consume alcohol or caffeine.

• Perform moderate exercises.

During Fasting

• Follow doctors instructions on how long to fast.

• Stay hydrated.

• Go on walks or perform light exercise.

• Stop fasting if you feel unwell.

• Drink teas or black coffee.

After Fasting

• Do not overeat.

• Eat proteins

• Eat carbs

Health Benefits

• Controls blood sugar.

• Fights body inflammation.

• Improves cardiovascular health.

• Weight loss improves.

• Prevents some types of cancer
